Rainwater Harvesting
Inner Mongolia is an arid area, and even though the old-fashioned drainage design method is still used, many rains and snowfall are lost. To this end, the designer uses the sponge city's underground water storage system, cubic lawn, and flat roadside stone for maximum rainwater harvesting.
Shunt Cold Current
In terms of climate, Hulunbeier is located in the northeastern part of China. The lowest temperature in winter is minus 40 degrees Celsius. It is also the first place in China to experience the Siberian cold current. The designer diverts the cold current from the northwest by setting up multiple cube lawns and walls.
Reduce Noise and Dust
In the small scale, the project site is located at the intersection, and there is a large plaza on the opposite side of the road for people to gather and activities. Therefore, the design of the project is mainly based on private space design, providing a small event venue.
Project Details

Client: Personal Entrustment

Date: 1th Februery, 2014

Online: Unknow

Project Background

The client requested to build a small private space at the intersection. Through the analysis and consideration of the site, the designer lists the following keywords as design metrics: water saving, reducing the effects of cold current, and isolating noise and dust.
